About me

I am an aerospace engineer with thermofluids experience in academia and industry, in the aerospace and power generation sectors.

Outside of work, my main focus is on photography and aviation. While I grew up having various contacts with photography, I only took it up as a serious hobby in 2020. As for aviation, I've been passionate about it from my childhood years. My goal with aviation photography is to share the beauty I see in what many consider mundane and commonplace in the 21st century.
