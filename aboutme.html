Who am I?

My name is Pierre. I am an aerospace engineer, with research and industrial experience centered around jet engines. Outside of work, I can typically be found in a field, camera in hand, photographing aircraft. This website serves to show you what comes of it; it is a tool to share my passion for the aerospace field with the world. I hope you enjoy having a look through the pictures on the website.
