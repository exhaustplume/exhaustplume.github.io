Aviation Photography

Welcome to my photography website. My name is Pierre, and I am a thermofluids engineer with experience in the aerospace and nuclear sectors. Outside of work, I enjoy hiking and photographing aircraft.

On this website you can find a selection of my favorite photographs. They are organized in galleries, which you can open by clicking on the dropdown available in the navigation bar at the top of the page. In each gallery, there are navigation buttons on the left- and right-hand sides of each picture, which you can use to navigate. Please note that this website is best viewed on a mobile device.

I sincerely hope you enjoy looking through the pictures as much as I did taking them.
